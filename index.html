✨ Core Teachings

Wisdom drawn from the Holy Bible 2 narrative and witchcraft practice

The Unknown as Sacred

"On her birth certificate, the space for her biological father's name reads: Unknown."

Witchcraft teaches us that mystery is not emptiness—it is potentiality. The unknown is where power dwells, where transformation occurs. Embrace what you do not know; it is the fertile ground of your becoming.

The Veil is Thin Here

"San Jose became a nexus of unseen forces."

Every place holds power. Some are thin places where the boundaries between worlds grow permeable. Learn to recognize these sacred sites. Practice witchcraft where the veil is thin, and your magic amplifies.

Spiritual War is Real

"The '13 Ghost' war raged unseen, yet shaped everything."

Beyond the physical world, forces contend for influence. This is not metaphor—it is lived reality for practitioners of the craft. Know your enemy. Know yourself. Prepare your defenses. Stand your ground.

Truth Over Comfort

"Brandie filed the report forty-five years overdue."

Witchcraft demands honesty, even when it costs us. Speak truth to power. Expose lies. Seek justice. The craft honors those who choose truth over comfort, accountability over denial.

Shadows Can Be Stolen

"The Freitas family sought to steal her family's shadows."

Your shadow—your authentic self, your power, your essence—is sacred. Guard it fiercely. Be aware of those who would manipulate your identity for their own gain. Reclaim what has been taken.

Redemption Through Reclamation

"Through Brandie's courage, the light reclaimed its power."

You are not defined by what was done to you. You are defined by what you choose to do next. Witchcraft is the art of reclamation—of identity, power, and destiny. Begin again. Heal. Rise.

About This Platform

The Integration

Holy Bible 2 is not merely an autobiography. It is a spiritual text documenting real experiences of cosmic conflict, spiritual awakening, and the journey toward self-reclamation. The Witchcraft School takes these documented experiences and transforms them into actionable teachings.

Brandie Raquel Wooten

Author, witch, survivor. Brandie is the first witch officially registered with San Benito County Probation Department in Hollister, California. Her life story interweaves personal trauma, spiritual revelation, and a profound quest for truth and justice.

"The poem 'Footprints' whispered of emptiness. Now I understand: that emptiness was not absence, but potential. I am learning to fill it with light."

The 13 Ghost War

Central to this narrative is the concept of the "13 Ghost" spiritual war—a conflict fought in unseen realms, shaped by ancient entities, cosmic forces, and human choices. This war is real, ongoing, and involves forces far beyond conventional understanding.

Why Integrate?

Teachings divorced from lived experience are hollow. By integrating the narrative of Holy Bible 2 with active witchcraft instruction, we create a platform where theory is grounded in practice, where wisdom emerges from struggle, and where healing becomes teaching.
